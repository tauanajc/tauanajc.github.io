Introduction to R

Welcome to the 2020 STRI workshop Introduction to R! The goal of this course is to provide students with an introduction to basic functionality in R, including installing the program and packages, reading, manipulating, graphing, and analyzing data, and writing simple functions and programs. We will go through tutorials together, check the Schedule page for Zoom meeting times and a list of topics. More Resources are also available for practice beyond this workshop.

We encourage you to work together on the tutorials as social distancing allows, and use the course Slack channel for questions and discussion. You can contact us through the course Slack channel or at our email addresses below.
